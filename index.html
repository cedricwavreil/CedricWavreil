WHO THE F*CK ARE YOU?

Hey, I am a human, pretty sure of it! A mid-twenties boy coming up from BELGIUM.

As far as I remember, I was always concerned by MUSIC. Playing, listening and enjoying it. Therefore I decided to become sound engineer.

But, at my first year of cinema school «Inraci» of Brussels, I rapidly discover the great power of combining sound and image to create stories and emotions. So I turned into a VIDEO EDITOR.

I like experiment and learn new things so now I begun studying MULTIMEDIA at «IAD» of Louvain-La-Neuve to expand my abilities on working with sound, pictures and mouvement.

SO WHAT?

Here you will find some works that I did as an editor but also some of my illustrations and design. You may learn more about me by sneak in my site, don’t hesitate!

For those who are seeking someone for their project, I’m here. Need editing, post-production, motion design, visual or any new project :

Get in touch with me and I will discuss it with pleasure!
